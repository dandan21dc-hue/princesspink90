AFTERDARK — CO-HOST AGREEMENT (TEMPLATE)
=========================================

This agreement is entered into between AFTERDARK ("Platform") and the
undersigned Co-Host ("Co-Host") as of the date signed below.

1. ROLE
   Co-Host will assist in planning, promoting, and running events on
   behalf of the Platform. Co-Host acts as an independent contractor,
   not an employee.

2. CONDUCT & CONSENT
   Co-Host agrees to uphold the Platform's Consent-First philosophy and
   Zero-Tolerance harassment policy at all times, and to complete any
   required safety, vetting, and health-screening steps.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Attendee identities, contact information, and any private details
   shared during events must be kept strictly confidential.

4. COMPENSATION
   Any compensation, revenue share, or reimbursements will be agreed
   separately in writing on a per-event basis.

5. TERMINATION
   Either party may end this arrangement at any time in writing. The
   Platform may revoke Co-Host status immediately for any conduct or
   safety violation.

6. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
   By signing below, Co-Host confirms they have read, understood, and
   agreed to this Agreement, the Platform's Community Standards, and
   the Consent & Safety policies.
